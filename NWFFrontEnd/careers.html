Careers

This isn't just an organization. This is a community. And we welcome you to be part of it. Join the team! Join the campaign!

Teams


Research Team

Our research team does research on opposing candidates, demographics, script creation, and other technical research oriented assignments.

Operations Team

Our Operations team conducts voter outreach operations through phone banking and canvassing. They also do translations for brochures, websites, and assist other departments in completing their tasks.

Data Analytics Team

Data analytics does campaign websites, social media, and works on the Machine Learning and data analysis aspect of our campaigns.

Advertising Team

Our advertising team handles our outreach to voters and creates literature campaign like brochures and flyers.



Position Levels and Job Listings


  • Associate level positions are available for all teams.

  • The VP of Advertising position is available.

  • The VP of Operations position is available.

  • Associate level positions are available only to high schoolers.

  • VP level positions are available only to college students.



To Apply:


Please send your Resume and Cover Letter to info@nwfstrategies.com with your name and the position you are applying for on the subject line.
